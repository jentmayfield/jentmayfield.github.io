Jennifer Torrez-Mayfield's First Web Page

"Hello, my name is Jennifer Mayfield I'm originally from Greeley, Colorado. I grew up as an Army brat and lived all over the place throughout my childhood. One of my father's last duty stations was at White Sands Missile Range in New Mexico. I spent my last two years of High School and College in Las Cruces, NM. This was also where my grandmother was originally from. My first job out of High School was a night auditor at the Las Cruces Hilton where I discovered my aptitude for numbers and pursued. Accounting. I had my first job as an auditor in Los Angeles, CA and worked on my MS in Accountancy from the University of Notre Dame.
I eventually settled in Austin, Texas and earned my CPA license in Texas. I'm currently an Assistant Controller for a nonprofit that helps the community find affordable housing. I enjoy volunteering in scouting as a Bear den leader for third graders. We host an exchange student from Japan. I also volunteering as a volunteer deputy registrar in Travis County. I enjoy spending time traveling with my husband, my two sons and step-daughter."
